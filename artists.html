Josh Bravener

Josh Bravener is a folk/country artist based out of Fredericton, NB. He pulls his inspiration from all sorts of music, from Hank to Clapton. Bravener writes his songs in a way that tells a story, taking the crowd on a journey through the life of his grandfather, father and himself. Bravener started performing at the young age of six, impersonating Elvis Presley with his father Mike. Josh and his father toured around Canada and the US competing and singing with Elvis' drummer, DJ Fontana. Josh starting writing his own tunes in high school and released his self titled EP in the summer of 2013.

Bailey Boyer & Katie Hanson

Bailey Boyer (grade 12) and Katie Hanson (grade 11) are both students Rothesay High School. Bailey has been an active member of the Saint John music community for many years along with her father, renowned musician Doug Boyer. She's been in several musical productions at RHS and will be playing the role of Taylor McKessie in RHS's upcoming production of High School Musical later this month. Katie has been in multiple musical productions with KV players, the Saint John theatre company and RHS. Most recently she has been a part of the RHS rendition of High School Musical as Sharpay Evans. Katie and Bailey have been performing together at events in Kennebecasis Valley for almost a year.
